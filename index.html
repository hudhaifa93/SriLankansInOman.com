About This Initiative

SriLankansInOman.com is a small passion project created by a Sri Lankan expat in Muscat. It’s being built slowly and thoughtfully to give our community one place to find useful information, feel connected, and access tools that make life a little easier here in Oman. More features and updates will be introduced as the project grows.

What to expect:

  • Useful resources and community-focused information relevant to Sri Lankans living in Oman.
  • Guides, experiences, and tips shared from real day-to-day life in Oman.
  • A gradually expanding platform with new sections added step-by-step.
  • Open invitations for collaborators, supporters, or anyone interested in contributing ideas to help grow this community hub.
