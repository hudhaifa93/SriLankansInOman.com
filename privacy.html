Privacy Policy

Last updated:

SriLankansInOman.com is a simple informational website built by a Sri Lankan expat in Oman. The platform does not operate accounts, payments, or automated messaging. It exists solely to share curated information and highlight marketplace categories for the community.

Information Collected

The website does not automatically collect personally identifiable information. The only data received is what you voluntarily provide when you send a WhatsApp message. The message content and contact details are used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Use of Analytics

Lightweight, privacy-friendly analytics tools may be used to understand page popularity, approximate locations (at country level), and devices used. These tools do not store personal profiles and only provide aggregated insights to improve the content experience.

Data Storage

WhatsApp chats are stored securely within the WhatsApp platform. No separate database or CRM exists for this website. If you request deletion of a message, it will be removed manually as soon as possible.

Third-Party Links

SriLankansInOman.com may reference third-party websites for housing, jobs, or classifieds. Once you leave this site, please review the privacy policies of those destinations. This website is not responsible for their practices.
